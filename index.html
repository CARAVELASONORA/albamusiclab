About

The Person Behind
the Music

I don’t see composing and production as a technical service — it’s a musical conversation. Fifteen years between the studio and the stage have taken me from scoring the feature film Avó e o Amor to orchestrating Rabo de Peixe for Netflix alongside composer Pedro Marques, and from full symphonic theatre scores with the Filarmonia das Beiras orchestra to solo piano work released under my own name.

My role isn’t just to hit record; it’s to listen to your vision and bring that same experience — the ear of an accompanist trained to serve someone else’s story — to help you find your voice. I’ve led live orchestral performances for audiences of 8,000+ and written continuous 77-minute scores played live to picture. No ego, just the music.

What we Do

Services

01 — Scoring & Composition

Musical Storytelling

Music serves the narrative. I work closely with directors and creators to find the specific musical pulse your scene needs, composing themes that speak where words fail.

02 — Production & Identity

Studio Production

This is a collaboration. We sit down, we deconstruct your ideas, and we find the musical identity that fits you. It’s about creating a safe space to experiment with arrangements until the music feels right.

03 — Live Direction

From Studio to Stage

Taking music to a live audience requires a different mindset. I help translate our studio arrangements into a live musical experience, ensuring you feel secure and powerful when you step on stage.

04 — The Learn

Sharing the Craft

No secrets, no gatekeeping. A space for us to sit down—online or in person—and discuss artistry, composition, and the reality of a career in music. Mentorship based on real-world experience.
