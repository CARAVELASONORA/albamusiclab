About

The Person Behind
the Music

I don’t view production as a technical service; I view it as a musical conversation. With years spent between the studio and the road—directing musicals, composing for TV, and playing alongside countless artists—I’ve learned that the best work comes from emotion, not just perfectly EQ'd frequencies.

My role isn’t just to hit record; it’s to listen to your vision and use my experience to help you find your voice. No ego, just the music.
